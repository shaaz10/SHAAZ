Frequently Asked Questions

Is my event covered immediately?

Yes, once your application is approved and payment is processed, your coverage begins instantly.

What documents do I need to provide?

Typically, we require event details, venue contracts, and identification proof. Our AI makes the upload process seamless.

How do I file a claim?

Simply log into your dashboard, select your active policy, and click "Raise Claim". You can track the status in real-time.

Can I cancel my policy?

Cancellations are possible up to 48 hours before the event start date. Please check the specific policy terms for details.
